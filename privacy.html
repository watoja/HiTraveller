HiTraveller Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 16, 2025

Your privacy is important to HiTraveller. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (the "Site").

Information We Collect

We collect information primarily to enhance your travel planning experience:

  • Personal Information: We collect your **username** (stored in your browser's local storage for sign-in) and **email address** (if you use our subscription form). We do not collect passwords or sensitive financial data.
  • Usage Data (Cookies & Local Storage): We use local storage to **remember your sign-in status** so you don't have to log in repeatedly. We use browser cookies to understand which pages are visited and how the site is used (e.g., Google Analytics, if implemented).

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To **maintain your session** (Sign In/Out functionality).
  • To send you **promotional and travel-related emails** (if you subscribe).
  • To **improve our website** layout, features, and content based on usage patterns.

Data Security

We use administrative and technical security measures designed to protect your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Websites

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., flight booking engines, hotel sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies directly.

Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@hitraveller.com
